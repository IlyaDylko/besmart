## 2. Information we process

### 2.1 Information stored on your device

The App stores learning progress and preferences locally on your device (for example via on-device storage), including roughly:

- Whether you finished onboarding  
- A local “premium” flag (placeholder until real billing ships)  
- Streak, XP, and daily-goal related values  
- Learning goal preference (e.g. curiosity, career, memory, conversation, habit)  
- IDs of completed lessons and book ideas  
- Ideas you opened or saw in the discover feed  
- Books marked as “reading now”

This data stays on your device unless you clear app data, uninstall the App, or use an in-app reset (if available). **We do not operate a BeSmart account backend that syncs this progress to our servers today.**

### 2.2 Information we do not intentionally collect as profile data

We do not ask you for:

- Full name, postal address, or government ID  
- Email address or phone number inside the App (unless you email us yourself)  
- Photos, contacts, microphone, or precise location for App features  

Quiz answers are used in the App experience and are **not** designed to be uploaded as personal quiz transcripts. Our analytics guidelines avoid logging quiz or slide text as event properties.

### 2.3 Information collected by analytics and advertising partners

When the App runs, third-party SDKs may collect technical and usage data as described in their policies, including for example:

- App opens, sessions, and approximate usage patterns  
- Device / app identifiers used for analytics or advertising measurement (subject to platform rules and your choices)  
- Events such as onboarding steps, paywall views, idea or lesson opens/completions, and similar product events  
- Content identifiers (e.g. book or lesson IDs), learning goal enums, and paywall “source” labels  

**Partners currently integrated:**

1. **Google Firebase Analytics (Google Analytics for Firebase / GA4)**  
   - Used for product analytics (funnels, engagement).  
   - On iOS, our Firebase Analytics configuration is set up **without AdSupport / IDFA** for Firebase.  
   - Google’s policies: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and Firebase/Google Analytics documentation.

2. **Meta Platforms (Facebook SDK — App Events)**  
   - Used to measure installs and in-app events for advertising and attribution (including standard event mappings where configured).  
   - On **iOS**, advertiser tracking is coordinated with **App Tracking Transparency**. If you deny tracking, we instruct the Meta SDK accordingly.  
   - On **Android**, advertising ID / related permissions may be used as allowed by the platform.  
   - Meta’s policy: [Meta Privacy Policy](https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy/).

These partners may process data on servers outside your country. Their independent privacy policies apply to their processing.

### 2.4 Information you give us directly

If you contact us by email or another channel you choose, we process the content of that communication (for example to answer support requests).

### 2.5 Permissions

| Permission | Platform | Purpose |
|------------|----------|---------|
| Tracking / ATT | iOS | Measure which ads help people discover BeSmart and improve ad performance. You can change this in iOS Settings. |
| Internet | Android / iOS | Required for analytics and ads SDKs to send events when online. |
| Advertising ID (where applicable) | Android | Used by ads measurement partners as allowed by Google Play policies. |

We do **not** currently request notification, camera, microphone, photo library, contacts, or precise location permissions for core App features.

We use information to:

- Provide and improve learning features (ideas, books, lessons, progress)  
- Understand product usage and fix issues

## 5. Sharing

We share information with:

- **Service providers / SDK vendors** listed above (Google, Meta) to run analytics and ads measurement  
- **Authorities** if required by law or to protect rights, safety, or the App

## 6. Retention

- **On-device progress** remains until you clear it, reset it in the App, or uninstall.  
- **Analytics / ads events** are retained by Google and Meta according to their retention settings and policies. We configure product events for product and marketing measurement, not indefinite personal profiling by BeSmart staff.

## 10. Your choices

- **iOS Tracking:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → BeSmart  
- **Android ads:** Device settings for ad personalization / advertising ID (varies by OS version)  
- **Reset local progress:** Use in-App reset controls if shown (e.g. Profile), or clear app storage / uninstall  
- **Opt out of Meta ads measurement:** Deny ATT on iOS; use platform advertising controls where available  
- **Contact us** to request help with privacy questions  

Uninstalling the App removes local App data from that device but does not automatically delete historical analytics events already received by Google or Meta.
